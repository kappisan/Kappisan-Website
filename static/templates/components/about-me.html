About Me

I like to learn new things and solve really difficult problems. I like the challenge of representing a portion of reality with logical symbols and systems. I have the ability to see patterns in abstract mathematical symbols. I will never substitute reality for elegance, nor will I give the people who use my model false comfort about its accuracy, instead I will make explicit its assumptions and oversights.

Currently living in Ipswich, England. I have a rich portfolio of interactive websites and API's. I have over 6 years professional experience in web development and many more years of hobby experience.
